# The Agentic Trio

<div class="flex justify-center gap-5 mt-4">
  <div class="bg-lightGray text-petrol rounded-full w-32 h-32 flex flex-col justify-center items-center"><div class="text-3xl mb-1">📋</div><b class="text-sm">Product</b></div>
  <div class="bg-lightGray text-petrol rounded-full w-32 h-32 flex flex-col justify-center items-center"><div class="text-3xl mb-1">🎨</div><b class="text-sm">UX / Design</b></div>
  <div class="bg-lightGray text-petrol rounded-full w-32 h-32 flex flex-col justify-center items-center"><div class="text-3xl mb-1">🛠️</div><b class="text-sm">Engineering</b></div>
</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-6 mt-8 max-w-4xl mx-auto">
  <div class="bg-lightGray text-petrol p-5">
    <div class="font-bold mb-1">How it used to be</div>
    <div class="text-sm">The engineer was stuck in delivery, with little time for discovery. A large team coordinated around it.</div>
  </div>
  <div class="bg-petrol text-white p-5">
    <div class="font-bold text-apricot mb-1">With agents</div>
    <div class="text-sm">Agents take the delivery grunt work, so all three actually do discovery together. Smaller team, bigger chunks.</div>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="mt-6 text-center text-base text-petrol">The exchange between these perspectives is the part we keep. Agents just change who has time for it.</div>
