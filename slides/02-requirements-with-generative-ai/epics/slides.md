# What are Epics?

<div class="space-y-6 text-xl leading-relaxed">
  <p><span class="font-bold">Origin:</span> Agile Software Development, popularized by the book <em>User Stories Applied</em> (Mike Cohn)</p>
  <p><span class="font-bold">Purpose:</span> Organize large bodies of work that are too big to complete in a single sprint, providing a high-level container for related user stories and tasks.</p>
  <p><span class="font-bold">Recommended Input:</span> User story maps, UX flows, proto-personas, technical requirements, business rules, acceptance criteria guidelines</p>
</div>
